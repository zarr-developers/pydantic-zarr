Removed hand-rolled type exports that are now provided by `zarr-metadata`: `NamedConfig`/`AnyNamedConfig` (replaced by `zarr_metadata.NamedConfigV3`), `RegularChunking`/`RegularChunkingConfig` (replaced by `zarr_metadata.RegularChunkGridMetadata`), `DefaultChunkKeyEncoding`/`V2ChunkKeyEncoding` and their Config variants (replaced by `zarr_metadata.DefaultChunkKeyEncodingMetadata`/`V2ChunkKeyEncodingMetadata`), `FillValue` union (replaced by `zarr_metadata.JSONValue` for loose specs, or the `Core`/`Extra` strict families for dtype-aware validation), `MemoryOrder` (replaced by `zarr_metadata.ArrayOrderV2`), `DimensionSeparator` (replaced by `zarr_metadata.ArrayDimensionSeparatorV2`), and `CodecDict` (replaced by `zarr_metadata.CodecMetadataV2`). This is a breaking change.
