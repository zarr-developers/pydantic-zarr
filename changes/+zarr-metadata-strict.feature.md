Added strict Zarr v3 specs (importable from `pydantic_zarr.v3`) in two families: **Core** (`CoreArraySpec`, per-dtype classes `CoreBoolArraySpec`/`CoreInt8ArraySpec`/`CoreFloat64ArraySpec`/etc., `AnyCoreArraySpec` discriminated union, `CoreGroupSpec`) and **Extra** (`ExtraArraySpec`, `ExtraBoolArraySpec`/`ExtraFloat64ArraySpec`/etc., `AnyExtraArraySpec`, `ExtraGroupSpec`). Both families enforce `fill_value` coupling to `data_type` (e.g. `float64` accepts `"NaN"` but `int64` does not) and reject unknown data types and codec strings. Core restricts chunk grids to `regular` and codecs to the seven core-spec types; Extra additionally accepts `rectilinear` chunk grids and `scale_offset`/`cast_value` codecs.
